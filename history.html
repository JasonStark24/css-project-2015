history


Rufina Eda C. Pagaura

owner

Rufina Eda C. Pagaura is a 39 yrs old businesswoman and was married to juanito M. Pagaura. On her past life, she was just working as a saleslady and her husband was just a tricycle driver. they started their small business "sari-sari" store with the help of her sister-in-law who gave her small capital to start a business. Over the years, their store started to earn profit and they started another business a small piggery with just 8 pigs at first (5 sow & 3 boar), But sadly their piggery didn't end well because of the complaints of their neighbors about the bad odor and many flies of their surrounding. So they think of another business and came up buying a land/lot and started gardening. But the workers she recruited is not consistent on their work, so she decided to increase their salary if they will continue to work hard and of course if they will not try their best on their work she will fire them or let them leave her farm.
